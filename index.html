How to read. All models scored on identical rows (same hour, station, day-ahead 12–36h, daylight). JMA MSM / ECMWF / GFS are raw public weather models — the same ones we feed into V14/V15 — so this shows what the ML ensemble adds on top of its own inputs. RMSE vs MAE: RMSE (default) is the industry standard and weights big misses heavily — a risk view; MAE is the plain average miss — closer to day-to-day trading cost.
