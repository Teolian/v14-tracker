Forecast explorer — contract komas

Each issue is the daily contract freeze (05:25 JST Kenes slot — one per day). Pick a freeze and a station: the table is that freeze’s committed delivery day as 30-min komas (daylight only; night/zero komas dropped, JST). Our forecast is hourly — each hour fills its 2 komas (as the contract does). V15 = our forecast; JMA = JMA MSM; Measured = JMA pyranometer once published. W/m². Sky = the koma’s weather from the clear-sky index kt (actual on finished days = measured÷clear-sky, else forecast = V15÷clear-sky): ☀️ clear (kt ≥ 0.65) · ⛅ variable · ☁️ overcast · 🌧️ rain.
